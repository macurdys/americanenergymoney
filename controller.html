Compute Futures

Qi / Quai Controller

The dual-token controller balances miner demand between Qi (energy-denominated) and Quai (market-priced). When miners prefer Qi, the controller increases kQuai to make Quai rewards more attractive — and vice versa. This is the mechanism that keeps Compute Futures anchored to physical reality.

Parameters
Blocks 500
Number of blocks to simulate
Qi Preference 65%
% of miners choosing Qi over Quai rewards
Conversion Penalty 5%
Discount imposed on token conversions — scales adjustment magnitude
Difficulty Growth 10%
Total difficulty increase over the simulation period
kQi Growth 20%
Predetermined kQi schedule growth (Moore's law proxy)
Init kQuai 2.21e17
Starting kQuai value in 1e16 wei
Mechanism

rewardQi = kQi × difficulty

rewardQuai = kQuai × log₂(difficulty)

kQuai(i) = kQuai(i−1) × [1 + dir × α × S × P]

α = 1/1000 · S = 3 when Qi popular, 1 otherwise · P = conversion penalty
