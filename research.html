Tracking live entropy

Bitcoin's Block Clock counts blocks. The Quai Clock counts what blocks actually represent — total thermodynamic work performed by the network, measured as cumulative entropy.

Entropy is the fundamental unit. Under Proof of Entropy Minima, each block's difficulty reflects provable entropy minimization. The clock aggregates this across the entire chain history — a running tally of physical computation that can never be faked or reversed.

This is the energy ledger. Every increment on the clock corresponds to real joules expended, real heat dissipated, real irreversible computation performed. It is the thermodynamic receipt for American energy converted into money.
